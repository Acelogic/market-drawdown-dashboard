Key Insight
When avg >> median, a few extreme crashes skew the data. The 2020s shows the biggest gap (-11.9% avg vs -2.5% median) — most years were calm, but 2020, 2022, and 2025 had major crashes. The 1970s also shows this pattern with the 1974 crash (-37.5%) pulling up the average.
